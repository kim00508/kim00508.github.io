About Me

I studied fashion design and previously worked in the fashion industry. My role as a fashion technologist involved specializing in fitting garments and creating garments blueprints, know as patterns in fashion, using CAD computer programs. I enjoyed my work working with many global retail brands such as GAP, ZARA, and so on.

Working in fashion was enjoyable, but I realized it might not be a long-term career path for me. I did not plan to leave the fashion industry forever. Instead, I wanted to broaden my skills for the future. So, I made the decision to enroll in a course on interactive media design to expand my skill set. My journey just started on the 6th of May 2024. Even though I am not sure how my previous career in fashion will align with there new skills in interactive media design, I am enjoying the journey.
